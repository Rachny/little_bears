Our Story

Born from a
mother's wish

LittleBear began in a small workshop in 2018, when founder Elena Hart couldn't find a toy soft enough, safe enough, and beautiful enough for her newborn. So she made one herself — and everything grew from there.

A small stitch at a time

What started as a single homemade bear for a nursery quickly caught the attention of friends and neighbors. Elena prioritized strict quality rules: zero plastics, 100% traceably-sourced organic cotton threads, and natural plant extracts for rich safe coloring dyes.

Eco-friendly & Conscious

Today, our collection has grown, but our process remains remarkably unchanged. We work entirely alongside local suppliers to minimize transport footprints and donate a portion of all proceeds directly to children's health and nature preservation foundations.
